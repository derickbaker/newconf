Conference Location

Cox Convention Center in Oklahoma City

About Oklahoma City

The DUG Midcontinent conference and exhibition launched in Tulsa, Oklahoma in 2013.

Over the past two years, this event has experience phenomenal growth — almost 60% growth in the exhibition and 25% growth in attendance. In an effort to sustain this growth trend, the event has relocated to the Cox Convention Center in the state’s capital – Oklahoma City – in 2015.

Explore Oklahoma City

Take advantage of all Oklahoma City has to offer! Whether you are looking for simple western charm or a busy metropolitan experience, Oklahoma City is the place to be. The city is filled with heritage and culture, boasting many local attractions, sporting events, restaurants and nightlife.
