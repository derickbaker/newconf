September 12th to 14th - San Antonio, Texas

At A Crossroads Looking Toward Tomorrow

Looking Toward Tomorrow

As signs of a market recovery take hold, industry leaders' questions are shifting from "how low will it go, and how long?" to "how much will it recover, and how quickly?" Texas' prolific Permian Basin is already experiencing a surge in activity.

This September, hear directly from 20+ senior-level executives and industry analysts in uniquely candid conference sessions. You'll meet and network with the region's top producers during the event's 9+ hours of dedicated networking sessions. Plus, hundreds of exhibiting companies will showcase the latest products and technologies on the trade show floor.

Register today   Why attend?

MIDSTREAM Texas event co-Located with DUG Eagle Ford

Hart Energy's MIDSTREAM Texas conference and exhibition explores midstream activity across the Lone Star State and beyond.

Get the latest updates from over 20 of the industry's most sought-after leaders, network with hundreds of upstream, midstream and downstream executives and explore the latest products and technologies from over top exhibitors. Walk away with new insights, proven best practices and contacts you can't make at other events.

Visit MidstreamTexas.com to learn more and register!

Are you interested in Exhibitor & Sponsorship Opportunities?

The DUG Eagle Ford exhibition hall is the perfect place to promote your brand. With the new co-located MIDSTREAM Texas conference, the shared exhibition hall is selling out quickly.

Speakers

  • Kirk Trosclair

    Fountain Quail Energy Services

    Mr. Trosclair brings more than 21 years of diversified energy experience to Fountain Quail. His background includes drilling and exploration, engineering design, oilfield fluid terminal operations, logistics management, construction, and business development. Prior to serving the company as Executive Vice President and COO, Mr. Trosclair served as Director of Terminal Operations for GreenHunter Resources, Inc. Mr. Trosclair received his B.A. and A.S. in Petroleum Engineering Technology from Nicholls State University.

  • M. Jason Pigott

    Chesapeake Energy Corp.

    M. Jason Pigott was appointed Executive Vice President – Operations, Southern Division in January 2015. Before joining Chesapeake in August 2013, Mr. Pigott worked at Anadarko Petroleum Corporation, where he served as General Manager for its Eagle Ford team from June to August 2013 and the General Manager for the East Texas and North Louisiana team from October 2010 to June 2013. Prior to these roles, Mr. Pigott was the Planning Manager for Anadarko's Southern and Appalachia Region and held a wide variety of positions focused on all aspects of developing unconventional resources. Mr. Pigott graduated from Texas A&M in 1997.
